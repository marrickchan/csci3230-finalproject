Classes

In Diablo 3, there are 6 main classes as shown below. Choose the one to your liking when you play!

  • Witch Doctor
  • Barbarian
  • Monk
  • Wizard
  • Demon Hunter
  • Crusader

Witch Doctor

Witch Doctors mostly fight with spells and pets.

Barbarian

Barbarians are great tank characters.

Monk

Monks are great at healing teammates and dealing devastating blows if necessary

Wizard

Wizards use spells to fight.

Demon Hunter

Demon Hunters use their bow or crossbow to rapidly clear enemies.

Crusader

Crusaders use their shield to gain the advantage in battle.

Legendary Gem Calculator

In Diablo 3, there are 21 Legendary Gems that give you special effects to increase your power. This calculator lets you see what Greater Rift Level you need to play to get what chance at leveling your gems.
